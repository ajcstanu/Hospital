About Us

Learn more about Kanjnetra Eye Hospital

Kanjnetra Eye Hospital is a leading eye care facility located in Anisabad, Patna. Established with a vision to provide world-class eye care services to the people of Bihar and neighboring regions, we combine cutting-edge technology with compassionate care.

Our team of experienced ophthalmologists, optometrists, and support staff are dedicated to preserving and restoring your vision with personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific needs.

Equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic and surgical equipment, we offer a comprehensive range of eye care services from routine eye examinations to complex surgical procedures.
